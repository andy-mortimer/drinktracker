Drink Tracker

You get what you measure!

Track how much you drink, both daily and over the weeks and months, on your iPhone or iPad.

It's easy to enter the exact strength and volume, and to re-use details you've already entered. You can even use a photo to quickly find and enter the same drink again.

If you're following particular consumption guidelines, such as the UK government guidelines, you can display those in the app too, and see at a glance whether you are on track or not.

Built-in templates

Templates with typical strengths and volumes are built into the app.

Use these directly, or as a starting point for entering exact information about each drink.

Log drinks as you go

Enter a drink's information once, then re-use it quickly with just a couple of taps.

Entering each drink as you start it means an accurate log, and no more scratching your head at the end of an evening to remember what you had.

If you'd rather enter the details later, you can add to and edit the record at any time.

Give details

Log the exact strength and volume of your drink to get an accurate picture of what you're drinking.

You can also take a photo, to help you recognise it the list when you log your next drink.

Current week at-a-glance

Quickly see how much you've drunk this week, compared to guidelines if you're displaying them.

You'll also see an estimate based on what you drank last week.

History

See how your drinking has changed over time.

Compare against guidelines

Compare your consumption against different iterations of the UK government guidelines, or just show the numbers.

Details

All units displayed in the app are UK units

Volumes are measured in millilitres

Universal app supports both iPhones and iPads
